The Blue Dwayne Johnson

The Blue Dwayne Johnson has no superhuman powers, but he is incredibly intelligent, having invented numerous weapons and high-tech vehicles that use such principles as robotics, anti-gravity, and zero-point energy, which he sold to black market buyers to make himself rich. He has a IceGun which fires in the colour blue.
